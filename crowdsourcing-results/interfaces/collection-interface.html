Instructions
Your task is to make a summary of an opinionated news article by selecting some of its text segments. The summary should be concise, self-contained, persuasive, and useful for others. The summary should comprise the thesis (what the author wants to persuade the reader of), and justification (statements that support this thesis, or provide background information about it).

Workflow:
  1. Read the following opinionated news article.
  2. Use the
    • thesis (1)
    • justification (2)
    • not in summary (3)
    -button to mark the segments as thesis, justification, or not in summary (default).
  3. Make sure your summary contains exactly a fifth of all segments. One to two of these must be marked as thesis. See "Final check" below.
  4. Answer the four multiple-choice questions at the bottom.
Hint: Save time by using 1, 2, and 3 on your keyboard if a dropdown menu is open.
