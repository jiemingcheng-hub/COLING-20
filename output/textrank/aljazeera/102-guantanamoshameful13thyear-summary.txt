The remaining 68 inmates are classified as "indefinite detainees", a Kafka-esque category used for detainees ineligible for either trial or release. Ameziane's forcible repatriation is only one of several other cases. In 2014, two other men were involuntarily repatriated to Libya. in 2007, two others were sent back to Tunisia.
