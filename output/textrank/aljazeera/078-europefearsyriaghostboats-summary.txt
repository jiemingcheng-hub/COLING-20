Last year some 230,000 people arrived illegally across the Mediterranean into the EU with Italy receiving the lion's share of 160,000 whilst 3,500 people died trying to make the crossing. The Syrians taking this journey are risking it all on what the UNHCR are calling the "most lethal route in the world". One refugee on board the Ezadeen put it succinctly when he told Italian Coast Guards that "we are alone and we have no one to help us". Finally of course we should never lose sight of the bigger picture. A new year gives Europe a chance to involve itself a new on addressing that most difficult of challenges
