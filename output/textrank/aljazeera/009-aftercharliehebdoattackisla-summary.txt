France's Prime Minister Manuel Valls has said that his country is now at war with radical Islam. What is "radical Islam"?. Is there such a thing?. Very few people know that in Islam there are two kinds of "jihad". But the greater jihad, which is less known, is the internal jihad - or struggle. 2 Timothy 4:7 states:"I have fought the good fight, I have finished the race, I have kept the faith.".
