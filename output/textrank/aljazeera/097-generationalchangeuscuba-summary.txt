The old guard of Cuban exiles, led by Senator Marco Rubio, predictably reacted with their usual anger. By contrast, many second generation Cuban Americans and advocates reacted differently - with optimism and hope for change. Those reforms have accelerated throughout the last decade, including an ongoing plan to unify the island's dual currency. European countries have been able to take advantage of those opportunities. Cuba's economy continues to search for growth. the government is actively looking outside the country for investment. Obama should be commended for basing policy on facts rather than emotions.
