Where can they run around unsupervised?. Yet almost everywhere they are designed out. In parliament's review of these plans, they aren't mentioned at all. Young people, around whom our lives should revolve, have been airbrushed from the planning system. I came across one exception - a street with wide grass verges. But only by comparison. It's an empty balancing pond, to catch water during exceptional floods. Children don't buy houses. so who cares?. But it's still not far enough. They impede it. What is good for them is bad for us.
